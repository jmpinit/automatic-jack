Automatic Jack

A cheap, modular scanning probe microscope platform

Work on this project has just begun. As an entry in the 2015 HackADay Prize the official project log can be found on hackaday.io.

Goals

  1. Image on an atomic scale.
  2. Do limited manipulations of matter on the nanoscale.
  3. Be at least as affordable and user-friendly as a mid-range hobbyist 3D printer.
